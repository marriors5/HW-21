Summary

We focused on a local non-profit organization focused around adopting shelter animals that deserve a second chance at life.
They are a dog-focused rescue that take them away from being victims of high-kill shelters. Unfortunately their website is out of date and my group and I came in, wondering how can we modernize the website while focusing on the aims of our foundation.
Our team was made of 4 people, I was involved in getting a little bit of research and ideation, I went out into the field to understand more about the organization and creating protoypes at the base level in which we elevated them. We've used Figma, Miro, Otter.io, Discord, Slack, Zoom, Lightroom, and a camera
It was in a tight 3 week stretch, scope had to change in the middle of our project because we realized that we were focusing on the adoption process but we admitted that we were surrendering that to Petfinder. We had to switch focus to the organization itself and motivate more involvement with visitors to be involved with the foundation

Research

We did 5 user interviews that my group members conducted, I just synthesize the information into their groups, since originally we did our research based off of the adoption process, it was messy later on to use that research We created Bryan at first, expecting this to be aimed towards families, especially with the father as the head. After doing the tests, we shifted it more so to a more professional feeling, we didn't focus too much on a persona since we have developed the concept that people get dogs due to companionship.

Ideation

Well we wanted to create a better, more simplified way for our members to get information in a coherent pattern. With our stakeholder, Anna, the founder of the foundation, she does state that she wants to promote more volunteers and donation based actions to support the organization.
